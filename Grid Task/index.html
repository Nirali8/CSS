Lion

The lion is a large cat of the genus Panthera, native to Africa and India. It has a muscular; a short, rounded head; and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.

Cat

The cat, commonly referred to as the domestic cat or house cat, is a small domesticated carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species of the family Felidae.

Raccon

The raccoon, also spelled racoon and sometimes called the common or northern raccoon to distinguish it from the other species, is a mammal native to North America
